

# Cashflow Dubai — Premium Mastermind Landing Page

## Overview
A dark-mode, ultra-premium landing page for a 3-day exclusive mastermind event in Dubai (March 21–23). Dark charcoal background (#0B0B0D), metallic gold accents (#D4AF37), cinematic feel, high-conversion architecture.

---

## Pages & Sections

### Landing Page (Single Page with Smooth Scroll)

**A) Sticky Navbar** — Transparent glass-morph on scroll, logo image, nav links (Program / Speakers / Experience / Pricing / FAQ), gold "Book Your Seat" CTA button.

**B) Hero Section** — Full-viewport dark cinematic background with gradient overlay, animated gold particle dots (CSS), bold condensed headline: "Dubai's Most Exclusive Private Business Mastermind", subheadline with event details, authority line for Nadir Doulfakkar, dual CTA buttons (Book Your Seat + Apply Now).

**C) Speakers Section** — "Founder-Led. Power-Curated." Large featured card for Nadir Doulfakkar with authority copy. 4 mystery speaker cards with gold glow borders, hover reveal animation, industry hints (no names).

**D) Program Timeline** — "Three Environments. One Elevated Circle." Vertical interactive timeline with 3 glass-morph day cards. Day 1: Villa, Day 2: Strategy, Day 3: Yacht. Each with detailed agenda items, gold accent separators, scroll-triggered animations.

**E) Value Stack** — "What Your Access Includes" — 3D-style tilt cards with hover depth effect showing: Private Member Card, All-Access Entry, Elite Networking, Trophy Recognition, Founder Sessions, Post-Event Access. Uses uploaded images (member card, trophy, merchandise) as visual references.

**F) Trophy Showcase** — "Recognition That Holds Weight" — Display the uploaded trophy images ($5K, $10K, $20K) in a spotlight section with premium pedestal styling and subtle rotation/glow effects.

**G) Pricing Section** — "Private Access Investment" — 5,000 AED displayed prominently. Inclusions list with checkmarks. Scarcity line about curated selection. Gold CTA: "Start Qualification".

**H) Qualification Form** — 6-question multi-step form with elite styling. Questions cover entrepreneurial level, 90-day objectives, motivation, revenue range, desired outcome, commitment readiness. Submitted locally (Supabase integration to be added later).

**I) FAQ Section** — Premium dark accordion with gold accents. Covers: who is this for, what's included, refund policy, location details, dress code, post-event access.

**J) Footer** — Minimal with gold separators, Dubai location, Cashflow Dubai branding, legal links.

---

## Design Details
- **Colors**: Background #0B0B0D, text white, accent gold #D4AF37
- **Typography**: Condensed modern sans-serif for headlines, clean body text
- **Cards**: Glass-morph with subtle gold borders, soft shadow layering
- **Buttons**: Gold fill primary, gold outline secondary, hover lift + glow
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-in, card tilt on hover (CSS transforms), gold shimmer effects, smooth section transitions
- **Mobile**: Vertical timeline, sticky bottom CTA bar, fully responsive

## Assets
- Logo image (uploaded) embedded in navbar and footer
- Trophy images ($5K bronze, $10K silver, $20K diamond) in showcase section
- Member card image in value stack
- Merchandise image as lifestyle reference
- T-shirt image as lifestyle reference

The qualification form will initially store submissions locally. Supabase + Stripe integration for approval flow, payment, ticket generation, and QR codes will be added as a follow-up step after the landing page is built and reviewed.
